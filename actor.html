SURYA IS MY FAVOURITE ACTOR

Surya, also known as Suriya, is a prominent Indian actor who primarily works in the Tamil film industry. Born on July 23, 1975, as Saravanan Sivakumar, he is one of the most successful and acclaimed actors in Tamil cinema. Surya made his acting debut in the 1997 film "Nerukku Ner," directed by Vasanth. However, it was his role in the 2001 film "Nandha," directed by Bala, that garnered him critical acclaim and recognition.
